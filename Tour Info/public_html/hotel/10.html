About Space Hotel

Located 200 m from Embassy of Turkey, Space Hotel and Apartments offers a restaurant, a fitness centre and air-conditioned accommodation with a patio and free WiFi. Featuring a kitchen with a microwave and a fridge, each unit also comes with a satellite flat-screen TV, ironing facilities, desk and a seating area with a sofa. There is a private bathroom with shower and a hairdryer in each unit, along with free toiletries. A continental breakfast is available daily at the aparthotel. Free private parking and a business centre are available, as well as a 24-hour front desk. A terrace can be found at Space Hotel and Apartments, along with a garden. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Embassy of Russian Federation, High Commission of Pakistan and Embassy of Italy. Gulshan is a great choice for travellers interested in business, culturally diverse food and friendly locals. We speak your language!
