InterContinental Dhaka



About InterContinental Dhaka

InterContinental Dhaka has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Dhaka. This 5-star hotel features free WiFi and a garden. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. All guest rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk. At the hotel, the rooms come with a seating area. Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at InterContinental Dhaka. The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Petrobangla is 1.4 km from InterContinental Dhaka, while University of Dhaka is 1.8 km from the property. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.2 for a two-person trip
