Ambala Inn



About Ambala Inn

Situated in Dhaka, 700 m from World University of Bangladesh, Ambala Inn features air-conditioned accommodation and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. All rooms come with a balcony. The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area. At Ambala Inn rooms have a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. A continental breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation. United International University is 1.7 km from Ambala Inn, while Nothern University Bangladesh is 2.1 km from the property.
