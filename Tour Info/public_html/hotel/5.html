Hotel Seven Star



About Hotel Seven Star

1.6 km from Jagannath University, Hotel Seven Star is located in Dhaka and features free WiFi and a tour desk. The property is around 2.1 km from Dhaka Stock Exchange, 2.3 km from Notre Dame University Bangladesh and 2.5 km from Supreme Court of Bangladesh. The accommodation provides room service, a shared lounge and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel, rooms come with a wardrobe. At Hotel Seven Star every room is fitted with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Bangladesh Bank is 2.5 km from the accommodation, while BUET is 2.6 km away.
