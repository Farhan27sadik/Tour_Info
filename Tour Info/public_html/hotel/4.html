GRACE 21



About GRACE 21

Offering a spa centre and fitness centre, GRACE 21 (SMART HOTEL) is set in Dhaka, 1.5 km from Biman Bangladesh Airlines. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site. Each room comes with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. GRACE 21 (SMAR HOTEL) is just 1.1 km from Hazrat Shahajalal International Airport and 1.5 km from Bashundhara Conference city ICCB. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.
