Platinum Residence



About Platinum Residence

Set in Dhaka, 500 m from Uttara University, Platinum Residence offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a shared lounge. Among the facilities at this property are babysitting service and a concierge service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel all rooms include air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a bidet, bathrobes and slippers. All guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. Platinum Residence offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The accommodation offers a terrace. Biman Bangladesh Airlines is 3.6 km from Platinum Residence, while IUBAT is 3.8 km from the property. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.1 for a two-person trip.
